Background

I am a 15 year old male. I am a decently fast speedcuber, which means I can solve the Rubik's Cube pretty fast. I also taught myself many programming languages, including, but not limited to: HTML, Swift, CSS, and C++.

Creating the Wiki

I'm pretty sure the idea for a wiki for PedestriaMC was already thought of, but I put it into action. It started as an idea in my head, when I was first invited to the server by bvsy. I then decided to document what happens in that server, but I quickly saw it was a waste of my time, and proceeded to leave the server. It was about two weeks later when I joined again. I then decided to start documenting it again. It was only a matter of time before I asked [Owner] wiicart if I could make a wiki for PedestriaMC. I went ahead and direct messaged [Owner] wiicart on Discord, asking if I could make a wiki for his Minecraft server. His answer is summed up by a yes. The next day I started my work on it and it took me about a half of a day to put together the file system and basic structure. He approved on how it was turning out and let me proceed with the project.
