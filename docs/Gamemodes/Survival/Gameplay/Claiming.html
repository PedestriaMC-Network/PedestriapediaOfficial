Claiming Land (Survival)

Basics

Tools Used

The tools that you will need are a golden shovel and a stick.

How to Claim Land

Before you start, you will want to put the Golden Shovel in your main hand. Then you will want to click a corner of what where you want to claim, and walk at least 5^2 blocks in a diagonal direction, then you will be able to click again, and you will have created a claim. The stick is basically used for checking if a piece land is already claimed. You do that by right clicking, then read chat.
